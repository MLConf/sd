Directions

The MLConf 2023 conference will be held at the Fairmont Grand Del Mar, a prestigious five-star hotel in San Diego. The hotel is located at 5300 Grand Del Mar Ct, San Diego, CA 92130.

Driving Directions

From Interstate 5:

  • Take exit 33 for Carmel Valley Rd toward State Route 56 E.
  • Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto Carmel Valley Rd.
  • Keep left, follow signs for CA-56 E.
  • Take exit 2 for Carmel Country Rd.
  • Turn left onto Grand Del Mar Way.
  • The Fairmont Grand Del Mar will be on your left.
