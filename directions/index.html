Directions

The MLConf 2023 conference will be held at the Fairmont Grand Del Mar, a prestigious five-star hotel in San Diego. The hotel is located at 1900 N. Harbor Drive, San Diego, CA 92101.

Driving Directions

From Interstate 5:

  • Take exit 52 for La Jolla Village Drive.
  • Turn left onto La Jolla Village Drive.
  • Continue for about 2 miles.
  • Turn right onto Torrey Pines Road.
  • Continue for about 1 mile.
  • The Fairmont Grand Del Mar will be on your left.

Public Transportation Directions

The Fairmont Grand Del Mar is served by the San Diego Trolley. The closest stop is the La Jolla Village Drive station. The station is located about a 10-minute walk from the hotel.

To get to the La Jolla Village Drive station from downtown San Diego, take the Blue Line Trolley. The Blue Line runs from downtown San Diego to La Jolla. The trip takes about 20 minutes.

To get to the La Jolla Village Drive station from the airport, take the Coaster train to Old Town Transit Center. The Coaster train runs from the airport to Old Town Transit Center. The trip takes about 30 minutes. From Old Town Transit Center, take the Blue Line Trolley to La Jolla Village Drive. The trip takes about 10 minutes.
